Online Ordering

Let the robots do all the work!

About 100 years ago if you wanted some carbide endmills you were pretty much out of luck because carbide endmills hadn't been invented yet.

Then 50 years ago you'd have to call up a distributor and they'd comb through a well-thumbed yearly catalogue to find exactly what you were looking for, put in the mail, and you'd get it... hopefully. If you wanted to track your package, get your invoice, see your account balance, there was a lot of calling, mailing, writing checks, and well... time consuming stuff.

Then came the internet. And we were one of the first cutting tool manufacturers to whole-heartedly embrace the web as an end-to-end ecommerce platform. Our website is over 10 years old!

Over the years we've accumulated functionality, so now you can see your discouted pricing online in CAD, USD, or EUR, purchase online, send in RFQs online. Want to know more? Keep scrolling. (And remember most of the really good stuff requires an account and a login.)

Live Pricing

Back away slowly from the calculator...

Want a run-of-the-mill list price? No problem. All our pricing is available on the site. Simply click on any part number or SKU on the site and you'll get the list price, right away.

But if you have a credit account with us, you already know it gets better than that. There are discounts.

Even better, if you login to the website, the pricing you see will be discounted just for you. Our best price, every time. No need to get out the calculate, no need to make a spreadsheet, just search for your part and voila.

Or, if you know our part numbers like the back of your hand or you simply want to copy and paste, you can use our Instant Order form.
