X-Mill: A variable helix rougher for any material

It took 2 years to develop the current X-Mill (with continuous improvements since then). We had to match the material to the geometry to the edge prep to the coating... We even bought our own coating machine to make this tool possible.

Application: High speed roughing, some finishing
Material: Cast Iron, Tool Steel, Carbon Steels
Environment: Flood coolant or air blast coolant
