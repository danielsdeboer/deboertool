Our People

We can't make magic without magicians.

CNC cutter grinder programmers are a rare breed. It takes attention to detail, lots of learning... and, well, lots more learning. Without the right people, all we've got is a bunch of fancy equipment sitting around doing nothing.

Since our inception we've done 100% in-house training. We've developed a rigorous CNC cutter grinder training program. And the training doesn't stop! We demand continual improvement, not just in machinery and technology but in operator expertise too.

In The History

Our Simarillion, somewhat abridged

In the early 1900s Opa de Boer left The Netherlands. This made a lot of people very happy and been widely regarded as a good move. He and his wife had several children. Several of them started businesses. This is one of those businesses.

In 2007 we moved to our current location at 336 Watline Ave, which we're currently concentrating on growing out of far sooner than we'd expected.
