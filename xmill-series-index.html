X-Mill Variable Helix

A roughing cutter for any material

It took 2 years to develop the current X-Mill (with continuous improvements since then). We had to match the material to the geometry to the edge prep to the coating... We even bought our own coating machine to make this tool possible.

Application: High speed roughing, some finishing
Material: Cast Iron, Tool Steel, Carbon Steels
Environment: Flood coolant or air blast coolant

X-Mill Regular

Flat Bottom, Radius, Ball Nose (inch sizes)

Our most popular X-Mill series. Standard lengths of cut and overall lengths. Great for most applications.

These X-Mills tend to hew quite closely to the overall length of our General Purpose line, however most have custom lengths of cut, designed to achieve optimal speeds and feeds in a variety of applications while minimizing harmonic vibrations.

View the X-Mill Regular product page

X-Mill Long Reach

Radius, Ball Nose (inch sizes)

For working in a tough-to-reach places. Extended lengths of reach with undersized necks. Feathered lead-out to minimise step-down machining marks.

Each of Long Reach X-Mill is designed with vibration minimization in mind, limiting the overall lengths and shortening the lengths of cut to create an optimal roughing environment for many materials.

X-Mill Metric

Radius (metric sizes)

We have a limited selection of Metric X-Mills available in standard sizes and lengths. These metric X-Mills have the same geometry and same proportional dimensions as their inch-sized counterpart. They are currently available only in Standard Length.
